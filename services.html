Nutrition Counseling


Are you ready to normalize your relationship with food and your body?

I offer virtual nutrition coaching in Austin, Texas that focuses on intuitive eating and nutrition through a non-diet approach. Virtual sessions are nutrition and body image coaching only. No medical nutrition therapy is provided virtually.

In our initial session, we’ll unpack your current relationship with food, your body, and self-care. We’ll work together to define your goals and create actionable steps to take charge of your health and happiness. In follow-up sessions, we’ll assess progress and tackle challenges together.


How long does nutrition counseling take?

Think about nutrition counseling like therapy – we’re not going to be able to cover everything in one session. If you’re like most of us, your brain probably has years and years of diet culture, food rules, and self-criticism ingrained. All this is going to require work and time to unpack and reframe. For most people, weekly meetings seem to provide the appropriate amount of time to implement new tools and strategies between sessions.
