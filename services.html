Nutrition Counseling


Are you ready to normalize your relationship with food and your body?

While I am based in Austin, TX, I offer virtual (video) nutrition sessions to anyone in Texas. Occassionally I can see folks residing in other states, but that is dependent on that state's licensing laws.

In our initial session, we’ll unpack your current relationship with food, your body, and self-care and start to define your goals. In follow-up sessions, we’ll work together to create actionable steps to take charge of your health and happiness. And session to session, we’ll assess progress and tackle challenges together.


How long does nutrition counseling take?

Think about nutrition counseling like therapy – we’re not going to be able to cover everything in one session. If you’re like most of us, your brain probably has years and years of diet culture, food rules, and self-criticism ingrained. All this is going to require work and time to unpack and reframe. For most people, weekly or bi-weekly meetings seem to provide the appropriate amount of time to implement new tools and strategies between sessions.
