ABOUT


Caroline Patrick MS RD LD

Nice to meet you! I am a registered dietitian (RD) on a mission to help women navigate their health in a supportive and sustainable way. I received both an undergraduate degree and graduate degree in Nutrition from Boston University. And, I’ve been working as an RD since 2016. In this time, I’ve worked in the fields of behavioral health, preventative health, disease management, health coaching, and eating disorders.

I love helping women out of cycles of chronic dieting as well as helping folks with various chronic disease states like diabetes, heart disease, and PCOS.

In my spare time, I like to road cycle, watch the Great British Bake Off, and find new hiking spots with my dog, Arlo.

If it sounds like we might be a good fit, I’d love to set up a discovery call or initial appointment to be sure (for both of us!).
